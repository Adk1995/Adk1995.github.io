Intel True VR

Intel True VR

Intel is at the cusp of a new revolution in the field of Virtual Reality. Until now, Virtual reality has mainly been just a completely immersive experience in a virtual world. However, Intel is trying to bridge the gap between the virtual aspect and real life moments. This new product by Intel captures various sports events such as NBA, MLB and even the Pyeongchang winter olympics in a panoramic field of view. There are millions of fans who would love to sit courtside at a game and watch their favorite team or player play. Unfortunately, due to either monetary costs or other reasons, this is not a viable option for many fans of the sport. This is where Intel True VR comes into the picture. With its wide capturing panoramic cameras, it captures all the moments of a sports event in a 180 degree panoramic view. Once the videos from the individual cameras are stitched together, the entire game can be viewed in VR from multiple positions. A fan can catch their favorite team play right from their couch with the same atmosphere and view as watching it from the sideline.
